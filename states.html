Choose the right place, we always take in count needs and preferences of the couple, we have several country states that meet the expectations for a wonderful wedding.

Contacting us we can take a tour of each of them so that you can choose the most appropriate one for your wedding. Below you will find information about the states we manage also we can suggest you the best one for you and your guests.



Amevea State

A spacious place surrounded by beautiful green areas and natural environments, where your wedding (Catholic, civil or Christian) will be wonderful, we have large parking and security areas, easy access by private car or taxi.

Remanso del Rio State

It is a perfect place for your wedding, with large beautiful gardens and a chapel, we are close to Bogotá, 45 minutes away, easy access. The place to make your wedding a dream.

Nogales State

We offer facilities that have large and comfortable spaces, ideal for celebrating your wedding from start to finish. You will find a beautiful chapel, where the ceremony can be carried out and a beautiful room for the reception, both specially designed for your wedding. You will also be able to enjoy the beautiful gardens that will serve as a setting for photographic recording.
